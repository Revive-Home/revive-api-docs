## Highlights

- Redesigned pre-estimate proposal pages with a new sidebar navigation layout, auto-hiding header, and deal-type-aware section ordering for Renovate to Sell and Sell 360 flows.
- New Sell 360 scenario comparison that presents conservative, target, and stretch financial projections side by side, with a downloadable PDF summary.
- Fully responsive mobile experience for pre-estimate proposals, including a slide-out navigation drawer and optimized slide layouts for smaller screens.

## Changes shipped to production

### revive-dashboard

- Redesigned pre-estimate proposal pages with a sidebar navigation layout, auto-hiding sticky header, and deal-type-aware section ordering. Sections now render dynamically based on whether the estimate is a Renovate to Sell or Sell 360 deal type. ([#2341](https://github.com/Revive-Home/revive-dashboard/pull/2341))
- Added a Sell 360 transaction summary section that displays three financial scenarios (conservative, target, stretch) with full cost breakdowns including initial payment, renovation budget, holding costs, program fees, and projected net proceeds. A comparison modal lets homeowners explore each scenario in detail. ([#2341](https://github.com/Revive-Home/revive-dashboard/pull/2341))
- Added a dedicated PDF page for Sell 360 scenario comparisons, allowing homeowners and agents to download a formatted summary of all three financial scenarios. ([#2341](https://github.com/Revive-Home/revive-dashboard/pull/2341))
- Added mobile-responsive pre-estimate experience with a collapsible slide-out navigation drawer, scroll-aware header behavior, and optimized slide layouts for all proposal sections. ([#2341](https://github.com/Revive-Home/revive-dashboard/pull/2341))
- Updated neighborhood comparables, renovation plan, case study, and value opportunity slides with refreshed layouts and improved content presentation across both deal types. ([#2341](https://github.com/Revive-Home/revive-dashboard/pull/2341))
