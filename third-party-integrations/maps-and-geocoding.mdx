## What it is

Mapping and geocoding integrations support map-based UX and location-derived workflows.

## Why we use it

Maps and geocoding improve address entry, property context, and location-based user experiences.

## Data flow

- Frontend apps call mapping/geocoding services from the client.

## Owner app(s)

- `admin` (in `revive-apps`)
- `dashboard` (in `revive-apps`)

## Relevant modules/endpoints

- `apps/admin/config/index.ts`
- `apps/dashboard/config/index.ts`
- `apps/api/src/config/index.js`
